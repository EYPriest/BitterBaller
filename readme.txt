Bitter Baller
Created By: Eli Priest
EYPriest@gmail.com
epriest.itch.io

Roll and jump over obstacles in this platformer. Keep Bob safe and try to get to the end. You can make the ball jump and you can jump again to make Bob jump, but be careful, Bob must land on the ball again to survive.

PC Controls: 

'Left' or 'a' to move Left
'Right' or 'd' to move Right
'Space' to jump and 'Space' again to double jump

Android Control:
Press the left side of the screen to move Left and Right
Press the right side of the screen to Jump

Bitter Baller was initially created for the Bitter Jam https://itch.io/jam/bitter-jam and used art assets provided by that jam
